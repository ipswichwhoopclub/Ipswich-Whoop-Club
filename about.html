OUR STORY

Ipswich Whoop Club was born from a simple idea: Ipswich deserves its own FPV racing community. What started as a handful of pilots flying in backyards and parks quickly grew into something bigger — a group of people genuinely passionate about indoor whoop racing and helping each other get better.

"We send it. Every pack."

In June 2026, we took the next step and became an Incorporated Association, giving the club a proper structure and the foundation to grow. We're currently searching for a permanent indoor venue in Ipswich to host regular race days, practice sessions and social meets.

Whether you're just getting started with your first tiny whoop or you're a seasoned racer chasing lap records, IWC is the place for you. We run a tight, friendly community with a focus on pilot progression and making racing accessible to everyone.
