IMPORTANT NOTES
🛡️  INSURANCE

Club membership includes coverage under the club's public liability insurance during sanctioned events. Members fly at their own risk at unsanctioned sessions.

📋  CODE OF CONDUCT

All members must abide by the IWC Code of Conduct and CASA drone regulations at all times. Membership may be revoked for serious breaches.

💰  FUTURE PRICING

Membership is currently free while the club is getting established. If fees are introduced in the future, all existing members will be given adequate notice and a grace period.
